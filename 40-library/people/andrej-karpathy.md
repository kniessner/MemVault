# Andrej Karpathy

**I like to train Deep Neural Nets on large datasets.**

Andrej Karpathy is a Slovak-Canadian computer scientist, AI researcher, and one of the most influential educators in deep learning. He was founding member of OpenAI, Director of Autopilot AI at Tesla, and is now running Eureka AI while building open-source LLM tools.

## Research Contributions

### Academic
- **Image Captioning with RNNs** (2015) — Showed CNN+LSTM works for image description
- **Deep Visual-Semantic Alignments** (2015) — Nearest-neighbor image retrieval with word vectors
- **ConvNetJS** (2013) — Browser-based neural nets, pre-TensorFlow.js

### Industry
- **Tesla Autopilot**: Built the neural net training infrastructure (Dojo precursor), pushed pure vision approach
- **OpenAI**: Early work on GPT-2/3, robotics, multi-modal models
- **Eureka AI**: Building AI-native education tools

### Philosophy
- "[Software 2.0](https://karpathy.medium.com/software-2-0-a64152b37c35)" (2017) — Neural nets are a new paradigm of programming

- "[The Unreasonable Effectiveness of Recurrent Neural Networks](http://karpathy.github.io/2015/05/21/rnn-effectiveness/)"
