## The Core Workflow

### Perplexity as Front-End Filter

Perplexity becomes the **entry point** for research:

1. **Query with sources** — Get structured, cited answers
2. **Extract insights** — Pull valuable findings
3. **Rewrite & contextualize** — Don't copy-paste; synthesize
4. **Store in Obsidian** — Add to vault with proper tags/links

### The Feedback Loop

```
Research Need → Perplexity Query → Synthesize → Obsidian Storage
      ↑                                              ↓
   New Insights ←——— Query Vault with Context ←——— Tags/Links
```

## Feeding Obsidian with Perplexity

### Practical Steps

| Step | Action | Purpose |
|------|--------|---------|
| 1 | Prompt Perplexity for structured output | Lists, comparisons, summaries |
| 2 | Review sources | Verify credibility |
| 3 | Extract key points | Don't capture everything |
| 4 | Rewrite in your own words | Deep processing |
| 5 | Tag and categorize | Maintain organization |
| 6 | Link to related notes | Build knowledge graph |

### Output Formats

Request from Perplexity:
- **Bullet lists** — Easy to parse
- **Comparisons** — Side-by-side analysis
- **Summaries** — Condensed insights
- **Source citations** — For verification

## Feeding Perplexity with Obsidian

### Contextual Research

Upload Obsidian notes to Perplexity (**Perplexity Spaces** or **upload feature**):

1. **Ground queries** — "Based on my notes about [topic]..."
2. **Ask follow-ups** — "What contradicts this?" / "What am I missing?"
3. **Gap analysis** — "What's not covered in my vault?"
4. **Synthesis** — "Connect these three concepts from my notes"

### Benefits

- **Context-aware answers** — Perplexity knows what you already know
- **Gap identification** — Discovers blind spots
- **Deeper connections** — Links concepts you hadn't connected
- **Quality control** — Flags outdated information in vault

## Limitations & Considerations

### Things to Keep in Mind

1. **Verification required** — Always check Perplexity sources
2. **Not all queries need saving** — Be selective
3. **Processing takes time** — Don't just archive; synthesize
4. **Privacy** — Uploaded notes go to Perplexity's servers
5. **Over-reliance risk** — Maintain critical thinking
