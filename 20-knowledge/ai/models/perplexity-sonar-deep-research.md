# Perplexity Sonar Deep Research

Research-focused model for multi-step retrieval, synthesis, and reasoning across complex topics.

## Best fit

- broad topic investigations
- source-heavy comparison work
- research tasks where the system should search, refine, and synthesize rather than answer from one pass

## Implications for vault use

- save first-pass outputs in `10-notes/40-resources/ai/`
- distill stable takeaways into provider, model, tool, and concept notes
- avoid treating the generated report as the final source of truth

## When not to use it

- simple fact lookup
- cases where raw search results are more important than synthesis
- lightweight queries where a fast grounded answer is enough
