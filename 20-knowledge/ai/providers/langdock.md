# Langdock

> Die Plattform für KI-Adoption — Enterprise AI deployment platform for German-speaking markets.

![Langdock](https://cdn.prod.website-files.com/69358127577725733df731fb/69741bc9d125e0b14cb16b2c_og.png)

## Overview

Langdock is a **German enterprise AI platform** focused on helping organizations securely deploy and adopt AI across their entire workforce. It provides a compliance-first approach (GDPR/DSGVO, ISO 27001) with model-agnostic architecture supporting major LLM providers.
