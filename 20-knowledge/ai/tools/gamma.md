# Gamma

> "Your all-in-one AI-powered design partner" — Effortless AI design for presentations, websites, and more. Your ideas are brilliant. The universe deserves to see them.

## Overview

Gamma is a **web-based AI platform** that generates presentations, documents, webpages, and other visual content using artificial intelligence. Founded in 2020 and valued at **$2.1B**, the platform serves **50+ million users** who create millions of pieces of content daily.

## Core Products

### 🎯 Presentations
Turn any idea into a polished slide deck with AI. Export to PPT, PDF, Google Slides.

### 📄 Documents
Structured, visual documents — from one-pagers to white papers — ready to share.

### 🌐 Websites
Generate shareable, hosted websites in minutes — no developers needed.

### 📱 Social Media
Generate platform-ready social content — sized, styled, and ready to post.

### 🎨 Graphics (AI Design Agent)
Create and edit custom, on-brand graphics — from infographics to illustrations.

### 🔌 API
Create with Gamma programmatically. Automate creation, integrate with tools, scale content.

## Workflow: Generate → Shape → Share

### 1. Generate
- Start with an idea, paste an outline, or import existing content
- 20+ AI models for highest-quality output
- Import your brand or use one of 100+ themes

### 2. Shape
- Instantly add smart layouts, rich content, and translations
- Generate images and rework with AI
- Collaborate real-time with your team

### 3. Share
- Export as PPT, PDF, PNG, or Google Slides
- Publish as a website or social post
- Track engagement analytics

### What are AI Credits?
Credits measure advanced AI usage:
- **Free plan**: All actions use credits
- **Paid plans**: Presentations and standard images included; credits used for Agent, premium models, API
- **Rollover**: Unused credits roll up to 2× monthly amount

## Pros & Cons

### Strengths
- ✅ **Multi-AI approach** — Uses best model for each task (20+ models)
- ✅ **Fast creation** — From idea to polished deck in minutes
- ✅ **Real-time collaboration** — Team editing simultaneously
- ✅ **Flexible outputs** — Presentations, websites, docs, social posts
- ✅ **Brand customization** — Import brand assets, custom fonts
- ✅ **API access** — Programmatic creation (Pro+)
- ✅ **SOC 2 Type II** compliant

### Limitations
- ❌ **No offline editing** — Requires internet connection
- ❌ **Limited animations** — Static slides (no PowerPoint-style animations)
- ❌ **Credit system** — Can be confusing; premium features consume credits
- ❌ **Template variety** — Fewer templates than Canva/PowerPoint
- ❌ **PowerPoint compatibility** — Exports may need manual adjustment

## History

Originally launched as a traditional presentation tool in 2020, Gamma initially "felt like a toy; no one was using it for real work" (co-founder admission). The company pivoted dramatically in **March 2023** by integrating AI generation features, which transformed it into a high-growth productivity platform. The New York Times described it as "a PowerPoint for the A.I. Era."
