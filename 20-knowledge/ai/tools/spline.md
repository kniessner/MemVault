# Spline

> "A design platform to create and collaborate on interactive production-ready experiences in real-time."

## Overview

**Spline** is a web-based 3D design and collaboration platform with integrated AI generation capabilities. It bridges the gap between designers and developers by enabling interactive 3D content creation directly in the browser, with exports to web, iOS, and Android.

| Attribute | Value |
|-----------|-------|
| **Type** | Web-based 3D Design Platform |
| **AI Features** | Text-to-3D, Image-to-3D (V1) |
| **Price** | Free to start; enterprise plans available |
| **Backed by** | Y Combinator |
| **Website** | https://spline.design |

## Key Features

### 3D Design & Modeling
- Real-time 3D rendering and modeling in the browser
- Primitives, vector networks, visual effects
- Physics, particles, and game controls
- Timeline animation and state management

### Hana Design Tool
- Canvas-based infinite workspace
- Vector networks and visual effects
- Variables, webhooks, and API data integration

### AI 3D Generation (V1)
- **Text to 3D** — Generate four variants from text prompts, convert to 3D models
- **Image to 3D** — Upload 2D front-facing images to generate 3D models
- **Remix & Reference** — Iterate on previous generations
- No modeling experience needed; generation completes in seconds
- Available from dashboard or directly within the Spline Editor

### Collaboration
- Real-time commenting and team invitations
- Link sharing and community remix platform

### Multi-Platform Export
- **Web** — HTML/JS, React, Next.js, Webflow, Framer, Wix Studio
- **iOS** — Swift / Xcode
- **Android** — Kotlin / APK / AAB

## Use Cases

- Interactive websites and landing pages
- Product design mockups and 3D visualizations
- 3D logos, icons, and branding assets
- Animated characters and gamified experiences
- Industrial and manufacturing visualization
